Social

You go to a concert and listen to live bands for the social experience of enjoying music with everyone else. With current streaming services, you're in your own world listening to your own music. We’re not here to provide another isolated music 'solution'. We're here to bring you a social experience that adds rather than takes away from the world.


Re-envisioned Feed

With a music sharing social network, we’ve been given a chance to innovate and have built this re-envisioned feed from the ground up with the sole purpose of music in mind. Aux Music’s feed acts as a playlist as well as a feed, streaming popular songs shared by those you are currently following.


Connect

On Aux Music, connecting and sharing with friends is fluid process - as it should be. When you sign up for Aux Music, you do it through facebook, so your friends will automatically be imported. To connect with more people, our connect section offers a chance to browse through people by mutual friends, search for celebrities, or people with similar tastes in music!

Any Platform

Aux Music is platform independent. Sign in with your favorite music streaming service and get access to all the same content.

Playlists

Getting around in some apps can be maddening, but with NeuBible’s spine navigation, it's easy to remember where you are. Plus, you’re always within a thumb’s reach of where you need to be, (and it's even easy to use with the larger screens of the iPhone 6 & 6 Plus).


Parties

Aux Parties are live collaborative playlists anyone in the party can add to. Type in a six character code to join a party and add your favorite songs to the playlist. The songs will stream in the order they’re listed, after upvotes are accounted for.


Aux Live

Aux Live is your personal radio. Create a live channel your friends can join. They’ll then be listening to what you’re listening to - perfectly synced. And vice-versa, if your friends are hosting a live channel, you will be able to tune in and listen to their songs.
